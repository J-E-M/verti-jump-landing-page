About Us

Verti-Jump is designed to help you bring your vertical jump height to the next level. Set goals, track your progress, and more on your journey to jump higher than ever before.

Set Goals

Verti-Jump allows users to set goals so you always have something to strive towards.

Track Progress

Verti-Jump tracks your progress throughout the program by displaying a graph that automatically updates based on your data to keep you motivated.

Take Charge

Only those who are ready to commit themselves to increase their jump height, and test the stength of their willpower will get the most out of this program. Never give up!
